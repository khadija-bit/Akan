Akan naming system

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Fruquently in Ghana, children are given their first name as 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.Here are Ghanian day names.

Male;
  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame
Female;
  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
